THE CORN RICE DISH.

A busy day is an exhausting day where you want to just go home and sleep through the night but you can't because your tummy is growling. Ordering food cost money. Making food is even more tiring but, if you have three simple ingridents at home. You can cook yourself a very yummy meal within matter of half an hour and be full while saving money.

HOW? I know you have this question in your mind. Well first off all go through your fridge and check if you have these three items.

  1. CORN
  2. RICE
  3. EGG (Optional But Recommended)

So... Do you have it?

Eggs are optional if you are a vegiterian else it is highly recommended that you don't skip it as most of our protien source will come from it. Eggs can be replaced with your preferred vegetables. Now that we have our ingridents. Let's start cooking.

    1. The Rice

      Add 2 cups white rice in 3Â½ cups water.
      Wash the rice till you see the rice in the water clearly.
      Slowly pour the water into the mixture while stirring.
      Raise the heat to high and stir in the rice; bring to a boil; cover and reduce heat to low.
      Simmer until the rice has absorbed all the moisture, about 30 minutes; fluff with a fork to serve.

    2. The Corn

      Take 14 ounces or 396 grams of corns; and wash it.
      Boil the corns till bubbles rise up.
      Set aside and let it warm.

    3. Eggs

      Take 2 eggs and scramble it.
      If not using eggs simply fry your veggies and set aside to cool it off.

  • Add 1 tablespoon of butter.
  • Add in the eggs with corn followed by the cooked rice.
  • Add spices to your liking.

And there you have it an very afforadable, enojayable meal easy to make.
